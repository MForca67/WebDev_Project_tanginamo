About AnimePinoy

What is the main topic/idea of your website?

To create a fun and interactive website for anime enjoyers such as a fandom website. It will contain music from the respective anime that are being included into the website, the website will mainly include popular anime because it should tailor to the general anime audience, as well as new watchers.

Who is your target audience?

Anime enjoyers in the age range between the ages of 15-25. We also expect newcomers into anime to check the website as it should act as a small gateway into anime so they can discover a new hobby of being an anime watcher.
